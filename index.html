Security & Compliance

Best practices for protecting healthcare data

Data Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Patient records are protected with additional layer of encryption.

Role-Based Access Control

Strict user role management ensures that patients, doctors, and administrators only access information relevant to their role and responsibilities.

GDPR & HIPAA Compliance

Our platform adheres to global healthcare data regulations including GDPR for EU citizens and HIPAA for US patient data protection.

Regular Security Audits

We conduct quarterly vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and security updates to protect against emerging threats.
